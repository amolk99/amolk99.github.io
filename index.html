About Me

Building bridges between
technology and design

I'm a Singaporean Computer Science graduate from Linnaeus University, Sweden, with a diverse background spanning robotics, machine learning, and software engineering.

My thesis work on Causal Discovery for Green Software Development involved statistical analysis and ML techniques to uncover dependencies in software energy consumption. This analytical mindset helps me approach design with data-driven precision.

I have a deep interest in design evolution, particularly following iOS's journey from skeuomorphism to flat design to the latest innovations. I believe great design is about understanding the "why" behind visual decisions.

Currently splitting time between Sweden and Finland, I thrive in multicultural environments and speak English, Hindi, and conversational Swedish.
